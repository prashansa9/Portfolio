Who We Are

Architectural Beauty (AB) is a custom formulation laboratory and marketing source. AB works with new or established brands. We are experts at product development in Skin Care, Hair Care, Bath and Body, spa, OTC, and baby products.

AB had worked with clients at every stage of development from startups to established retail skincare/color cosmetic companies well as those on QVC, HSN, and shop NBC. We have a well proven track record in skincare, haircare and makeup.

We are the perfect partners for retailers, package companies, contract manufactures, raw material vendors, seeking new innovative formulations. AB works well with individuals who want to create their own brand and bring them to market.

Our services reach beyond just formulation. We can provide services from product conception through scale up, manufacturingand distribution. Our collaboration with a product testing (claims, testimonials, and before and after picture development)

Our fully staffed 3000 sq. ft. facility is located in Paramus, NJ.
